Flaky Playwright tests in CI are a common challenge. It’s important to note that timing issues often cause toast notifications to fail intermittently.

To comprehensively address this, you should leverage robust waiting strategies, ensure your selectors are resilient, and consider the differences between local and CI environments. Furthermore, implementing best practices around retries and network idle states can elevate test stability.

In conclusion, take a holistic approach: stabilize selectors, wait properly, and monitor CI resources. This will foster more reliable end-to-end coverage going forward.
