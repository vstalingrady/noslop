# Writing modes

Pick intensity. **Default ship mode = balanced.**

| Mode | Who it's for | Do | Don't |
|------|----------------|-----|--------|
| **modest** | Letters, notes, anything that should feel unforced | Light anchors if they fit; natural digression; incomplete thoughts OK; end without a bow | Arc-toy dump (frame + twist + multi-scene aftermath + theme line); score farming; staccato “skill show” |
| **balanced** | **Ship default** — most agent writing | Anchors that help; one real mess or open cost; no sermon close; ban/glue clean; readable first | Chase VOICE 9+; force every craft checkbox |
| **max** | Research / stress tests only | Full craft pressure: dense anchors, deliberate unevenness, hard anti-sermon, optional StoryScope constructions if asked | Present as normal product voice without labeling research |

## Score expectations (guide, not law)

- **modest** — VOICE may sit mid; honest StoryScope often near book band (~0.1–0.3). That is OK.
- **balanced** — VOICE pass without hard_fail; do **not** require 9+.
- **max** — Numbers can go high; document the readability cost. Not the hero path.

## Control arm (evals only)

**default** = raw model-slop control (glue, fog, sermon). Not a skill mode — comparison only.

## PRE-WRITE

Always include:

```text
Mode: modest | balanced | max
```

If omitted, treat as **balanced**.
