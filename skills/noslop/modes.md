# Writing modes

Pick craft pressure. **Default ship mode = balanced.**  
Modes change **how hard** you press craft, not **what** the piece is about. Topic = user brief ([SKILL.md](SKILL.md) Subject ≠ method).  
Paper: [paper.md](paper.md) · Construction: [construction.md](construction.md).

| Mode | Who it's for | Do | Don't |
|------|----------------|-----|--------|
| **modest** | Letters, notes, unforced tone | Light anchors; digression OK; incomplete thoughts OK; end without a bow | Arc-toy dump; staccato “skill show”; novel construction on short prose |
| **balanced** | **Ship default** — most agent writing | Anchors that help; **sparse** 2–4 construction moves on long fiction; diversity seed; one real mess or open cost; no sermon; ban/glue clean; **readable first** | Chase VOICE 9+; force every checkbox; force StoryScope stacks; stack full palette |
| **max** | Research / stress tests only | Full craft pressure; may over-apply paper constructions on **long fiction**; document cost | Present as normal product voice; treat highest VOICE or P(human) as win |

## Expectations

- **modest** — mid VOICE is fine if the page is easy to finish.
- **balanced** — no hard_fail; sparse construction; do not require 9+; ship on page judgment.
- **max** — numbers can look strong; **readability cost expected**. Not the hero path. Over-stacking is a research stress, not product voice.

High VOICE / high StoryScope with stiff craft = **failure**. Scoremax = fail.

## Eval control arm

**default** = raw model slop (glue, fog, sermon). Comparison only — not a skill mode.

## PRE-WRITE

```text
Mode: modest | balanced | max
```

Omitted → **balanced**.
